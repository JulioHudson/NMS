New patch, No Man's Sky Frontiers!

No Man's Sky Frontiers is the game's latest free update. The update encourages players to explore planetary settlements, help them grow, and even found their own settlement by becoming an overseer. There are hundreds of new base parts to collect, including decorative parts, as well as a new expedition season called Cartographers on the horizon.

This trailer gives us a closer look at some of the new towns, while showing off No Man Sky Frontiers' improved visual effects. In addition, there will also be more in-game No Man's Sky rewards that will be available by watching others stream the game on Twitch.
